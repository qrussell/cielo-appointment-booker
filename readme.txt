A builder-focused appointment booking plugin that integrates with WooCommerce, syncs with Google Calendar, supports AJAX, and works natively with Divi. Designed for freelancers, agencies, and DIY entrepreneurs.

== Description ==

Cielo Appointment Booker is a lightweight yet powerful plugin built for builders, freelancers, and agencies who want full control over their scheduling workflows. It calculates pricing based on square footage, sends transactional emails with confirmation links, and integrates seamlessly with WooCommerce and Google Calendar.

Whether you're booking inspections, consultations, or service calls, this plugin gives you the tools to automate, personalize, and scale your appointment system.
